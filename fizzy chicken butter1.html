Our Philosophy

Sometimes Home Isn't a Place. It's a Taste.

There are days when all you miss is a familiar aroma. The fragrance of freshly cooked biryani filling the house. The sound of hot naan coming straight from the tandoor. The rich, buttery curry your family gathered around after a long day. Those moments become memories, and memories become the flavors we spend a lifetime searching for.

At Fizzy's Butter Chicken, we don't simply cook food. We recreate the feeling of sitting around a family table where every meal is prepared with authentic Pakistani and Indian spices, fresh halal ingredients, and recipes passed down through generations.

Whether you're sharing dinner with family, celebrating with friends, or simply craving the taste of home, our kitchen welcomes you with warmth, tradition, and unforgettable flavors. Every meal is served with care because great food doesn't just satisfy hunger, it creates memories that bring people back.

2016

A kitchen with no gas line

Ember & Oak opened in a converted foundry building with a deliberate limitation: no gas, no electric burners, nothing but three wood-fed hearths. It forced a different kind of cooking — one where the fire dictates the pace of the night, not the ticket printer.

A decade of restaurant experience across open-flame kitchens shaped that first menu, but the real education has come from thousands of dinner services since — learning what a fire wants to do, and getting out of its way.
